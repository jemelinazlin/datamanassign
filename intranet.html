Intranet

Category: Private Networks

An intranet is a private network used within an organization to securely share information, resources, and communication tools among employees.

It uses standard Internet technologies such as HTTP, but access is restricted only to authorized internal users, making it secure and efficient for internal communication.

Intranets are commonly used in schools, companies, and government institutions for sharing documents, announcements, and internal systems.
