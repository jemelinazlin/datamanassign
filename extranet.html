Extranet

Category: Network Systems

An extranet is a controlled private network that allows external users such as customers, partners, or suppliers to access specific parts of an organization’s internal system.

It extends an intranet by providing secure access to trusted outsiders while still protecting the organization’s private data and internal operations.

Extranets are commonly used in businesses to improve communication, share resources, and collaborate with external stakeholders without exposing the full internal network.
